Insturctions

  • Choose the time as instructed from the dropdown list.
  • Click the "Start Quiz" button.
  • Choose the correct answer for each question by clicking on it from the list.
  • Submission will be completed by two methods:
    • By clicking the "Submit" button or
    • Automatic submission when the timer is up.
  • You can see your score and the correct answers on the right column.
  • Questions that were answered incorrectly will be highlighted in RED, while correct answers will be in GREEN of the unanswered questions will be shown in ORANGE color.

DO NOT REFRESH OR CLOSE THE BROWSER BEFORE COMPLETING THE EXERCISE.
